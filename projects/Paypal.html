PayPal Consumer Services

Enabling future PayPal products

Overview

Moving away from PayPal legacy monolithic codebase to modular micro-services which will not only allow the company to iteratively improve current products faster but also iteratively build more customer centric products by taking in regular customer feedback .

Team

I was part of the team comprising of PMs, Designers, Product Owners and engineers which were responsible for designing and developing the micro-services which support existing products but also enable future PayPal products.

Role

I was the lead Software Engineer and led the development and architectural design of multiple APIs/Services.

Challenge

How might we move away from the monolithic PayPal codebase to modular services which will not only allow the company to iteratively improve current products faster but also iteratively build more customer centric products by taking in regular customer feedback .

Approach

Understanding existing system and products

Impact

Change in company culture to a more customer centric and experimentation driven.

  • Complete overhaul of the PayPal.com and mobile experience.
  • We were able to cut down the time for launch and release times from months to few hours.
  • Launch of multiple new products like PayPal.me in record time.

Reflections

My experience at Paypal was hugely rewarding as I got the opportunity to work and build many products which are still being used by hundreds of millions of people across the globe. I also got the opportunity to collaborate with many amazing people from many countries in an agile settings and learnt a lot.
