Crowded Contexts

Summer Internship

Overview

Service Design summer internship in Lisbon at Flour Consulting, where I worked on a creating a platform for their client CelFocus for crowded environments like music festival, conferences and sporting events.

Team & Duration

Tiago Abrantes, Inês Goncalves | 3 Months

Role

Service Design Intern

Tools

Sketch, Framer, Premier

Methods

Field Interviews, competitor analysis, fly-on-the-wall observations, market research

 

Brief

To understand the behaviour and needs of people at crowded environments like music festival, conferences, sporting events to create a platform for the attendees and businesses present in that environment.

Secondary Research

Understanding current market and technology trends for crowded events

I conducted secondary research to understand the events domains, stakeholders involved, market and technology trends. Few trends which stood out were:

  • Personalised events for attendees
  • Social media Engagement before, suring and after events
  • Experiential marketing to engage attendees
  • Convergence of events at festivals
  • Activism during events
  • Use of new technology like RFIDs, VR, and AR.
  • Mobile platforms for event management and marketing

Field Interviews and Observation

10+ days field research in 3 music festivals

We conducted fly in the wall observations, interviews with both attendees and people representing different businesses at Meo Sudoeste, NOS Alive, and Vodafone Paredes de Coura festival, which were held at different locations in Portugal.

The key questions we were interested in were:

  • How do people discover these events and what are the key factors in their decisions to attend a particular event?
  • How do people spend their time at the festival?
  • Do people use any of the events app, and if so how and why?
  • How do people interact in their groups and how do they interact with strangers?
  • Which businesses are succesfully able to attract attendees and why?
  • How much do people post or engage on social media during these festivals?
  • What are the painpoints during these crowded events and why?
